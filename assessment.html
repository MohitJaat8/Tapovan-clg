Progressive Assessment Tool for Children With C.P. (P.A.T.-C.W.C.P.)

Child Activity Assessment

Answer the following questions:

  1. Can the child sit without support? Yes No
  2. Can the child hold a spoon independently? Yes No
  3. Can the child communicate basic needs verbally? Yes No
  4. Can the child walk without assistance? Yes No
  5. Can the child recognize family members? Yes No
  6. Can the child follow simple instructions? Yes No
  7. Can the child pick up small objects using fingers? Yes No
  8. Can the child dress independently? Yes No
  9. Can the child express emotions appropriately? Yes No
  10. Can the child maintain eye contact during conversations? Yes No
  11. Can the child engage in simple play activities? Yes No
  12. Can the child understand and respond to their name? Yes No
  13. Can the child hold and use a pencil correctly? Yes No
  14. Can the child recognize basic shapes and colors? Yes No
  15. Can the child balance on one foot for a few seconds? Yes No
  16. Can the child eat independently without assistance? Yes No
  17. Can the child use the toilet independently? Yes No
  18. Can the child recognize and interact with peers? Yes No
  19. Can the child understand and follow a daily routine? Yes No
  20. Can the child respond appropriately to social interactions? Yes No
